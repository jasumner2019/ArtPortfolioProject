I'm a Tech Enthusiast, Family Man, and of course, Artist. I picked up drawing when I took an art class in college, where studying under an experienced professor really taught me perception, patience, and attention to detail. Since then, I've been drawing as my hobby and have now decided to make my talent available to the world!

My style of art can be summed up in the following phrase: 'The closer you look, the more you see.' I love pouring over meticulous details of a work of art, and then after a few hours taking a step back and admiring how each piece fits into the larger picture.

When I'm doing portraits, I choose to work in pencil. A human face is among the most beautiful, majestic, complex things that God created, and trying to capture one on paper for me requires a handy eraser and plenty of revision. However, in art I've heard it said that the very thing you should draw is the thing you believe to be unattainable, something you don't think you could capture. Attempt the impossible, and you'll surprise yourself every time. And so, I do portraits. If you're interested in a portrait, please reach out to me directly.
